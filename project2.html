Inspiration

In today’s fast-paced dining culture, customers often want quick and reliable insights into a restaurant’s best dishes. Restaurant owners and managers similarly face challenges in processing hundreds of customer reviews. This application allows users to interact directly with reviews to uncover popular dishes and make informed dining decisions.

What it does

Our application allows users to explore restaurant reviews using Redpanda Connect (OpenAI) to extract meaningful insights. Simply enter the restaurant's name, and the app will show top-rated menu items, personalized dish recommendations, and customer satisfaction trends.

How we built it

The application integrates Redpanda, MongoDB, OpenAI, Pinecone, and React. Reviews are stored in MongoDB, processed through Redpanda for real-time analysis. OpenAI transforms reviews into vector embeddings, which are stored in Pinecone for efficient retrieval of relevant reviews.

Results & Future scope

Users can make informed dining decisions quickly. This application can be expanded to other industries, such as retail or e-commerce, for similar review analysis and insight extraction.
